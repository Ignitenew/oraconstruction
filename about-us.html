About Us

ORA Construction is a trusted construction company serving homeowners and commercial property owners across Syosset with dependable building and renovation services. With more than two decades of industry experience, the company has established a reputation for delivering high-quality workmanship, practical solutions, and consistent results on projects of varying sizes and complexity. The team combines skilled craftsmanship with hands-on project management to ensure that every project is completed with precision, efficiency, and attention to detail.

The company provides a wide range of construction services designed to enhance both the functionality and appearance of residential and commercial properties. Its expertise includes demolition, interior and exterior remodeling, and improvements to key living areas such as kitchens, bathrooms, and basements. ORA Construction also specializes in building extensions and dormers that expand usable space and improve the overall value of a property. In addition, the company undertakes concrete work such as sidewalks and driveways, along with large-scale construction projects that involve building new homes from the initial layout through the final finishing stages.

What sets ORA Construction apart is its commitment to delivering projects that reflect both durability and thoughtful design. Every project is approached with careful planning and close collaboration with clients to ensure their vision is accurately translated into a finished result. By combining industry knowledge with reliable construction practices, the company is able to manage projects from concept to completion while maintaining high standards of quality and safety.

As a fully licensed and insured contractor, ORA Construction prioritizes professionalism and accountability in every aspect of its work. Clients rely on the company not only for its technical expertise but also for its transparent communication and dedication to meeting project timelines. Through years of hands-on experience and a strong focus on craftsmanship, ORA Construction continues to help property owners transform their spaces into functional, well-built environments designed to stand the test of time.
